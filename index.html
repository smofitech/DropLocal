Why use DropLocal?

Ditch the cables and the cloud. Here is how we make offline sharing a breeze.

No Internet, No Limits

Direct Wi-Fi or Hotspot transfer. No internet, no data usage, and no size limits. Perfect for rural areas, flights, or when saving mobile data.

Scan & Send

Instant connection via QR Code. Simply scan the receiver's screen, select files of any type, and transfer instantly without configuration.

Privacy & Security

100% offline-first. Your files never pass through external servers, meaning your confidential data stays completely private and secure on your local network.

How to Connect?

DropLocal works locally by establishing a secure direct link. Choose your configuration below.

Option B: Same Wi-Fi

Connect both devices to the same Wi-Fi network. Recommended for homes, offices, and cafes.

  • 1

    Join Network: Check that your sending and receiving devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi SSID.

  • 2

    Open App: Start DropLocal on both devices. Click 'Receive' on the target device to generate its IP & QR code.

  • 3

    Scan and Send: Click 'Send' on the sender device, scan the receiver's QR Code (or type their IP manually), and watch it transfer!

Option A: Hotspot

Turn on Hotspot on one device and connect the second to it. Ideal when outdoors, on a train, or when no local Wi-Fi router is available.

  • 1

    Enable Hotspot: Turn on mobile hotspot on one device (it doesn't need internet/data enabled).

  • 2

    Connect Second Device: Join the newly created hotspot network from the settings of your second device.

  • 3

    Transfer Directly: Open DropLocal on both devices, scan the QR code to pair, and start sharing at local speeds of up to 50MB/s!
